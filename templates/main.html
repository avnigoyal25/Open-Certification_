    WHAT IS OPEN CERTIFICATION ?

    Open Certification is a groundbreaking project developed to revolutionize the way certificates are generated for participants and winners of events. With the aim of alleviating the intricate process of certificate design, this innovative solution is the brainchild of the talented members of the OPEN Community at UPES Dehradun. Open Certification eliminates the painstaking tasks of crafting and formatting certificates by offering a streamlined, one-click approach. Now, event organizers can effortlessly create certificates of exceptional quality, freeing them from the complexities of design work. This project is set to redefine the certificate generation landscape, making it easier than ever to recognize and appreciate the achievements of event participants.
